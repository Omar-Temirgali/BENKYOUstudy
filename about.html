About Us


Founded in September of 2020 and based in Almaty, Kazakhstan, BENKYOUstudy is an educational center where you can take Japanese language courses at any level. This site provides a free online course to help you master the A1 level to learn, improve and share your language skills around the world - online or from a mobile phone or a tablet. You can always continue learning the language and sign up for courses at a very affordable price.


Our office is also located in Tokyo, where foreigners who want to learn the language can combine study and travel. There are over 240 students at the moment. We also help with employment in cities such as Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto and Sapporo.
